The Hidden Costs of Cloud AI

What every executive discovers too late about cloud dependency

Data Ownership Crisis

Every query you send trains your competitors' AI. Your proprietary insights become their competitive advantage. Major cloud providers explicitly reserve rights to use your data for model improvement.

Runaway Costs

API costs scale exponentially with success. Companies report $100K+ monthly bills that appeared overnight. Token limits force rationing of your own AI capabilities during peak business periods.

Security Vulnerabilities

Your sensitive data travels through multiple networks, servers, and jurisdictions. Data breaches at cloud providers expose millions of customers simultaneously. You have zero control over security incidents.

Performance Bottlenecks

Network latency adds 200-500ms to every request. Rate limits throttle performance during high-demand periods. Regional outages can completely halt operations for hours or days.

Vendor Lock-In

Switching costs become prohibitive as dependence deepens. Proprietary APIs and formats trap you in ecosystems. Pricing power shifts entirely to providers who know you can't leave.

Compliance Nightmares

GDPR, HIPAA, SOX, and other regulations become impossible to satisfy when data crosses borders. Audit trails disappear into cloud provider black boxes. Regulatory fines can exceed AI savings by 100x.
