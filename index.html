Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Omega

How quickly can I get started with Omega?

Most clients are operational within 24-48 hours of delivery. Our white glove service includes unboxing, setup, network configuration, and hands-on training for your team.

What AI models can Omega run?

Omega supports all major open-source models including Llama 3, Mistral, Mixtral, CodeLlama, and more. You can also fine-tune models on your own data or deploy custom models.

How does Omega ensure data security?

Your data never leaves your premises. Omega features end-to-end encryption, air-gap capability, and meets HIPAA, SOX, and FedRAMP compliance standards out of the box.

What if I need to scale beyond one Omega?

Omega is designed to scale. Our orchestration software allows multiple units to work together seamlessly. Add capacity as you grow without disrupting operations.

What kind of support is included?

Every Omega includes comprehensive support ranging from business hours to 24/7 coverage depending on your package. We also provide regular software updates and optimization services.
